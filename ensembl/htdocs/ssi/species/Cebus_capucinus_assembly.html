The Cebus_imitator-1.0 assembly was submitted by McDonnell Genome Institute - Washington University School of Medicine on 2016/04/04 . The assembly is on the Scaffold level, consisting of 140,597 assembled into 7,156 scaffolds. The N50 size is the length such that 50% of the assembled genome lies in blocks of the N50 size or longer. The N50 length for the contigs is 41,196 while the scaffold N50 is 5,274,112 The genome assembly represented here corresponds to GenBank Assembly ID: GCA_001604975.1
