The ICAR-NRCP-G1 assembly was submitted by ICAR National Research Centre on Pig and last updated on 2024-12-18. The assembly is on the chromosome level, consisting of 386442 contigs assembled into 25394 scaffolds. The N50 size is the length such that 50% of the assembled genome lies in blocks of the N50 size or longer. The N50 length for the contigs is 11062 while the scaffold N50 is 143437317.
