The Caty_1.0 assembly was submitted by Human Genome Sequencing Center - BCM on 2015/03/19 . The assembly is on the Scaffold level, consisting of 76,752 assembled into 11,433 scaffolds. The N50 size is the length such that 50% of the assembled genome lies in blocks of the N50 size or longer. The N50 length for the contigs is 112,942 while the scaffold N50 is 12,849,131 The genome assembly represented here corresponds to GenBank Assembly ID: GCA_000955945.1
