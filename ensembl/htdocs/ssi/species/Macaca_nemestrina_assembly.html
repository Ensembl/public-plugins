The Mnem_1.0 assembly was submitted by Baylor College of Medicine on 2015/03/19 . The assembly is on the Scaffold level, consisting of 94,057 assembled into 9,733 scaffolds. The N50 size is the length such that 50% of the assembled genome lies in blocks of the N50 size or longer. The N50 length for the contigs is 106,897 while the scaffold N50 is 15,219,753 The genome assembly represented here corresponds to GenBank Assembly ID: GCA_000956065.1
