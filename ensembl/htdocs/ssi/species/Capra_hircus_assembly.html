The ARS1 assembly was submitted by USDA ARS on 24/08/2016. The assembly is on the chromosome level, consisting of 30,399 contigs assembled into 29,907 scaffolds. The N50 size is the length such that 50% of the assembled genome lies in blocks of the N50 size or longer. The N50 length for the contigs is 26,244,591 bp while the scaffold N50 is 87,277,232 bp.
