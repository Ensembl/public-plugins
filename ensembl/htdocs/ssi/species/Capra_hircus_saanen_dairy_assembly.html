The Saanen_v1 assembly was submitted by Northwest A&F University and last updated on 2023-01-27. The assembly is on the chromosome level, consisting of 1531 contigs assembled into 1362 scaffolds. The N50 size is the length such that 50% of the assembled genome lies in blocks of the N50 size or longer. The N50 length for the contigs is 46208332 while the scaffold N50 is 102383509.
