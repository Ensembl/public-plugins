The EquAss-T2T_v2 assembly was submitted by University of Kentucky and last updated on 2025-06-27. The assembly is on the chromosome level, consisting of 855 contigs assembled into 848 scaffolds. The N50 size is the length such that 50% of the assembled genome lies in blocks of the N50 size or longer. The N50 length for the contigs is 102829910 while the scaffold N50 is 101318199.
