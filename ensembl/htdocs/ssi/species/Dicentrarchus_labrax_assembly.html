The dlabrax2021 assembly was submitted by Hellenic Centre for Marine Research, Institute for Marine Biology, Biotechnology and Aquaculture, Heraklion, Crete, Greece on April 2021. The assembly is on scaffold level, consisting of 574 contigs assembled into 302 scaffolds. The N50 size is the length such that 50% of the assembled genome lies in blocks of the N50 size or longer. The N50 length for the contigs is 12,677,075 while the scaffold N50 is 29,885,359.
