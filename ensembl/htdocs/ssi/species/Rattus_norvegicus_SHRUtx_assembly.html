The UTH_Rnor_SHR_Utx assembly was submitted by University of Kentucky and last updated on 2023-02-18. The assembly is on the chromosome level, consisting of 3407 contigs assembled into 2151 scaffolds. The N50 size is the length such that 50% of the assembled genome lies in blocks of the N50 size or longer. The N50 length for the contigs is 24520032 while the scaffold N50 is 136651366.
