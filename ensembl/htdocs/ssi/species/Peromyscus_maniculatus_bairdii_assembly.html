The Pman_1.0 assembly was submitted by Baylor College of Medicine on 2013/12/03 . The assembly is on the Scaffold level, consisting of 212,962 assembled into 30,921 scaffolds. The N50 size is the length such that 50% of the assembled genome lies in blocks of the N50 size or longer. The N50 length for the contigs is 36,367 while the scaffold N50 is 3,760,915.
