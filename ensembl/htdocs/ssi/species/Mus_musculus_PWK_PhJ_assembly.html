The assembly for PWK/PhJ was generated as part of The Mouse Genomes Project [https://www.mousegenomes.org/], additional strains can be found in Ensembl [https://www.ensembl.org/Mus_musculus/Info/Strains].

The assembly is on the chromosome level, consisting of 412 contigs assembled into 208 scaffolds. The N50 size is the length such that 50% of the assembled genome lies in blocks of the N50 size or longer. The N50 length for the contigs is 26698258 while the scaffold N50 is 126992800.
