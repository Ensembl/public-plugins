The assembly for CBA/J was generated as part of The Mouse Genomes Project , additional strains can be found in Ensembl.

The assembly is on the chromosome level, consisting of 544 contigs assembled into 143 scaffolds. The N50 size is the length such that 50% of the assembled genome lies in blocks of the N50 size or longer. The N50 length for the contigs is 12206296 while the scaffold N50 is 126990816.
