The NWAFU_Friesian_1.0 assembly was submitted by Academy of Gansu Yuansheng Zhongxin Dairy Sheep Industry and last updated on 2021-06-09. The assembly is on the chromosome level, consisting of 972 contigs assembled into 937 scaffolds. The N50 size is the length such that 50% of the assembled genome lies in blocks of the N50 size or longer. The N50 length for the contigs is 85264699 while the scaffold N50 is 96203338.
