The UU_Cfam_GSD_1.0 assembly was submitted by Uppsala University and last updated on 2026-03-26. The assembly is on the chromosome level, consisting of 2783 contigs assembled into 2198 scaffolds. The N50 size is the length such that 50% of the assembled genome lies in blocks of the N50 size or longer. The N50 length for the contigs is 14840767 while the scaffold N50 is 64299765.
