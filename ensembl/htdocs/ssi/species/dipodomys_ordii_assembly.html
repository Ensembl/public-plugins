The Dord_2.0 assembly was submitted by Baylor College of Medicine on 2014/12/12 . The assembly is on the Scaffold level, consisting of 148,226 assembled into 65,193 scaffolds. The N50 size is the length such that 50% of the assembled genome lies in blocks of the N50 size or longer. The N50 length for the contigs is 48,087 while the scaffold N50 is 11,931,245 The genome assembly represented here corresponds to GenBank Assembly ID: GCA_000151885.2
