The UTH_Rnor_WKY_Bbb_1.0 assembly was submitted by Inbred Rat Genome Sequencing Project (UTH/UK/UofL) and last updated on 2023-02-18. The assembly is on the chromosome level, consisting of 5009 contigs assembled into 3553 scaffolds. The N50 size is the length such that 50% of the assembled genome lies in blocks of the N50 size or longer. The N50 length for the contigs is 27772074 while the scaffold N50 is 139005698.
