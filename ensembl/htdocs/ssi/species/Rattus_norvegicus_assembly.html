The GRCr8 assembly was submitted by Genome Reference Consortium and last updated on 2024-01-31. The assembly is on the chromosome level, consisting of 297 contigs assembled into 77 scaffolds. The N50 size is the length such that 50% of the assembled genome lies in blocks of the N50 size or longer. The N50 length for the contigs is 64250101 while the scaffold N50 is 137014596.
