The Dog10K_Boxer_Tasha assembly was submitted by Dog Genome Sequencing Consortium and last updated on 2022-05-20. The assembly is on the chromosome level, consisting of 1162 contigs assembled into 147 scaffolds. The N50 size is the length such that 50% of the assembled genome lies in blocks of the N50 size or longer. The N50 length for the contigs is 27487084 while the scaffold N50 is 63738581.
