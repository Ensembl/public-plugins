The Felis_catus_9.0 assembly was submitted by Genome Sequencing Center (GSC) at Washington University (WashU) School of Medicine and last updated on 2019-11-21. The assembly is on the chromosome level, consisting of 4908 contigs assembled into 4524 scaffolds. The N50 size is the length such that 50% of the assembled genome lies in blocks of the N50 size or longer. The N50 length for the contigs is 41915695 while the scaffold N50 is 83967707.
