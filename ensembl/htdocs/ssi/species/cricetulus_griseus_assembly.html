The CriGri_1.0 assembly was submitted by Beijing Genomics Institute on 2011/08/23 . The assembly is on the Scaffold level, consisting of 265,787 assembled into 109,152 scaffolds. The N50 size is the length such that 50% of the assembled genome lies in blocks of the N50 size or longer. The N50 length for the contigs is 39,361 while the scaffold N50 is 1,147,233 The genome assembly represented here corresponds to GenBank Assembly ID: GCA_000223135.1
