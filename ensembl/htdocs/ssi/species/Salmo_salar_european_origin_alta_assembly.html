The Ssal_ALTA assembly was submitted by NORWEGIAN UNIVERSITY OF LIFE SCIENCES and last updated on 2022-05-12. The assembly is on the chromosome level, consisting of 4067 contigs assembled into 4042 scaffolds. The N50 size is the length such that 50% of the assembled genome lies in blocks of the N50 size or longer. The N50 length for the contigs is 19218354 while the scaffold N50 is 19218354.
