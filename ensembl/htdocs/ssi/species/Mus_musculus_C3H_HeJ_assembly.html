The assembly for C3H/HeJ was generated as part of The Mouse Genomes Project , additional strains can be found in Ensembl.

The assembly is on the chromosome level, consisting of 752 contigs assembled into 171 scaffolds. The N50 size is the length such that 50% of the assembled genome lies in blocks of the N50 size or longer. The N50 length for the contigs is 8562414 while the scaffold N50 is 127227482.
