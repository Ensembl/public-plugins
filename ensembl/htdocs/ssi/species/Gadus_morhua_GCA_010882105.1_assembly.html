The gadMor_Celtic assembly was submitted by CIGENE and last updated on 2023-01-19. The assembly is on the chromosome level, consisting of 1253 contigs assembled into 1127 scaffolds. The N50 size is the length such that 50% of the assembled genome lies in blocks of the N50 size or longer. The N50 length for the contigs is 10559872 while the scaffold N50 is 27362751.
