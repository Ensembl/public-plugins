The CavAp1.0 assembly was submitted by Leibniz Institute for Zoo and Wildlife research on 2014/01/21 . The assembly is on the Scaffold level, consisting of 2,319,938 assembled into 3,131 scaffolds. The N50 size is the length such that 50% of the assembled genome lies in blocks of the N50 size or longer. The N50 length for the contigs is 1,039 while the scaffold N50 is 27,928,671 The genome assembly represented here corresponds to GenBank Assembly ID: GCA_000688575.1
