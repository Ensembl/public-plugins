The USMARCv1.0 assembly was submitted by USDA ARS and last updated on 2022-12-29. The assembly is on the chromosome level, consisting of 14818 contigs assembled into 14157 scaffolds. The N50 size is the length such that 50% of the assembled genome lies in blocks of the N50 size or longer. The N50 length for the contigs is 6372407 while the scaffold N50 is 131458098.
