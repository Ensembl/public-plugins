The Tarsius_syrichta-2.0.1 assembly was submitted by Washington University (WashU) on 2013/09/18 . The assembly is on the Scaffold level, consisting of 492,903 assembled into 337,189 scaffolds. The N50 size is the length such that 50% of the assembled genome lies in blocks of the N50 size or longer. The N50 length for the contigs is 38,165 while the scaffold N50 is 401,181 The genome assembly represented here corresponds to GenBank Assembly ID: GCA_000164805.2
