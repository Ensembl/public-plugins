The Camarhynchus_parvulus_V1.1 assembly was submitted by Uppsala Univeristy on March 2020. The assembly is on chromosome level, consisting of 1,980 contigs assembled into 1,756 scaffolds. From these sequences, 30 chromosomes have been built. The N50 size is the length such that 50% of the assembled genome lies in blocks of the N50 size or longer. The N50 length for the contigs is 15,143,611 while the scaffold N50 is 71,061,686.
