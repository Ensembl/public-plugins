The Nleu_3.0 assembly was submitted by Gibbon Genome Sequencing Consortium on 2012/12/08 . The assembly is on the Chromosome level, consisting of 197,900 assembled into 17,524 scaffolds. The N50 size is the length such that 50% of the assembled genome lies in blocks of the N50 size or longer. The N50 length for the contigs is 35,148 while the scaffold N50 is 52,956,880.
