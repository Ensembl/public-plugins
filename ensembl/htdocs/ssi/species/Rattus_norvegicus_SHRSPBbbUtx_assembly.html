The UTH_Rnor_SHRSP_BbbUtx_1.0 assembly was submitted by Inbred Rat Genome Sequencing Project (UTH/UK/UofL) and last updated on 2025-11-26. The assembly is on the chromosome level, consisting of 4344 contigs assembled into 2734 scaffolds. The N50 size is the length such that 50% of the assembled genome lies in blocks of the N50 size or longer. The N50 length for the contigs is 50481003 while the scaffold N50 is 138881597.
