The Anan_2.0 assembly was submitted by Baylor College of Medicine on 2017/06/09 . The assembly is on the Scaffold level, consisting of 112,851 assembled into 28,922 scaffolds. The N50 size is the length such that 50% of the assembled genome lies in blocks of the N50 size or longer. The N50 length for the contigs is 126,456 while the scaffold N50 is 8,268,663 The genome assembly represented here corresponds to GenBank Assembly ID: GCA_000952055.2
