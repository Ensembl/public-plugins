The Panu_3.0 assembly was submitted by Human Genome Sequencing Center on 2017/04/20 . The assembly is on the Chromosome level, consisting of 118,811 assembled into 71,135 scaffolds. The N50 size is the length such that 50% of the assembled genome lies in blocks of the N50 size or longer. The N50 length for the contigs is 149,817 while the scaffold N50 is 585,721 The genome assembly represented here corresponds to GenBank Assembly ID: GCA_000264685.2
