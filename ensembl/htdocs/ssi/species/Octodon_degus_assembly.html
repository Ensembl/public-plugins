The OctDeg1.0 assembly was submitted by Broad Institute on 2012/05/01 . The assembly is on the Scaffold level, consisting of 259,905 assembled into 7,135 scaffolds. The N50 size is the length such that 50% of the assembled genome lies in blocks of the N50 size or longer. The N50 length for the contigs is 19,847 while the scaffold N50 is 12,091,372.
