The Ninghe_Sus_1 assembly was submitted by China Agricultural University and last updated on 2023-01-29. The assembly is on the chromosome level, consisting of 1660 contigs assembled into 70 scaffolds. The N50 size is the length such that 50% of the assembled genome lies in blocks of the N50 size or longer. The N50 length for the contigs is 4265198 while the scaffold N50 is 137575542.
