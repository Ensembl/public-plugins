The CVASU_BBG_1.0 assembly was submitted by Bangladesh Goat Genome Consortium (BGGC) and last updated on 2023-01-10. The assembly is on the chromosome level, consisting of 3696314 contigs assembled into 3972 scaffolds. The N50 size is the length such that 50% of the assembled genome lies in blocks of the N50 size or longer. The N50 length for the contigs is 819 while the scaffold N50 is 102339471.
