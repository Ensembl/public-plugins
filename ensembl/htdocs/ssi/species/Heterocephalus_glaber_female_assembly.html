The HetGla_female_1.0 assembly was submitted by Broad Institute on 2012/02/17 . The assembly is on the Scaffold level, consisting of 114,653 assembled into 4,229 scaffolds. The N50 size is the length such that 50% of the assembled genome lies in blocks of the N50 size or longer. The N50 length for the contigs is 47,778 while the scaffold N50 is 20,532,749.
