The Mmur_3.0 assembly was submitted by Baylor College of Medicine on 2017/02/06 . The assembly is on the Chromosome level, consisting of 50,984 assembled into 7,678 scaffolds. The N50 size is the length such that 50% of the assembled genome lies in blocks of the N50 size or longer. The N50 length for the contigs is 210,702 while the scaffold N50 is 108,171,978 The genome assembly represented here corresponds to GenBank Assembly ID: GCA_000165445.3
