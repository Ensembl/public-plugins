The Macaca_fascicularis_5.0 assembly was submitted by Washington University (WashU) on 2013/06/12 . The assembly is on the Chromosome level, consisting of 87,764 assembled into 7,625 scaffolds. The N50 size is the length such that 50% of the assembled genome lies in blocks of the N50 size or longer. The N50 length for the contigs is 86,040 while the scaffold N50 is 88,649,475 The genome assembly represented here corresponds to GenBank Assembly ID: GCA_000364345.1
