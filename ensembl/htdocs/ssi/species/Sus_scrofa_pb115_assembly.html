The CAU-K assembly was submitted by China Agricultural University and last updated on 2021-07-20. The assembly is on the scaffold level, consisting of 34590 contigs assembled into 13384 scaffolds. The N50 size is the length such that 50% of the assembled genome lies in blocks of the N50 size or longer. The N50 length for the contigs is 223517 while the scaffold N50 is 30528819.
