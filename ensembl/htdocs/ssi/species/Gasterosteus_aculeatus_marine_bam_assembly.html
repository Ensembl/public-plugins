The BAM assembly was submitted by Institute of Ecology and Evolution and last updated on 2019-06-11. The assembly is on the scaffold level, consisting of 45706 contigs assembled into 25430 scaffolds. The N50 size is the length such that 50% of the assembled genome lies in blocks of the N50 size or longer. The N50 length for the contigs is 24675 while the scaffold N50 is 491022.
