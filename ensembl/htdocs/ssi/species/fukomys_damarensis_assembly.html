The DMR_v1.0 assembly was submitted by BGI on 2014/09/04 . The assembly is on the Scaffold level, consisting of 162,545 assembled into 74,730 scaffolds. The N50 size is the length such that 50% of the assembled genome lies in blocks of the N50 size or longer. The N50 length for the contigs is 44,830 while the scaffold N50 is 5,314,287 The genome assembly represented here corresponds to GenBank Assembly ID: GCA_000743615.1
