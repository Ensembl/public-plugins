The NRF assembly was submitted by NORWEGIAN UNIVERSITY OF LIFE SCIENCES and last updated on 2024-08-16. The assembly is on the chromosome level, consisting of 716 contigs assembled into 716 scaffolds. The N50 size is the length such that 50% of the assembled genome lies in blocks of the N50 size or longer. The N50 length for the contigs is 93255103 while the scaffold N50 is 93255103.
