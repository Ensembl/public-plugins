Outreach

The Outreach team is the main contact point for Ensembl users. We answer questions sent via email, make sure that problems spotted by users are fixed by liaising with the other Ensembl teams, prepare the online courses, help pages and tutorials that you can find on the Ensembl website, and give workshops, demos and presentations on the use of Ensembl worldwide.

We make efforts to engage in communication using social media as well as training. We'd love to hear from you on:

  • The Ensembl Helpdesk YouTube channel
  • Train Online
  • The Blog
  • Facebook
  • Twitter (#Ensembl)
