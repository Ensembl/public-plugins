The ButJap1.0 assembly was submitted by GEROMICS on 4 February 2020. The assembly is on scaffold level, consisting of 127,054 contigs assembled into 29,199 scaffolds. The N50 size is the length such that 50% of the assembled genome lies in blocks of the N50 size or longer. The N50 length for the contigs is 36,723 while the scaffold N50 is 7,468,869.
