The ASM966780v1 assembly was submitted by Central China Normal University on November 2019. The assembly is on chromosome level, consisting of 8,584 contigs assembled into 5,302 scaffolds. From these sequences, 13 chromosomes have been built. The N50 size is the length such that 50% of the assembled genome lies in blocks of the N50 size or longer. The N50 length for the contigs is 1,946,319 while the scaffold N50 is 394,693,044.
