Processing steps for other vertebrates

The following pages describe more about Ensembl Regulation's computational process used to produce regulatory annotation for vertebrates other than human and mouse.

Our primary data contains both single-end and paired-end reads, which are processed slightly differently, but the basic steps are the same: preprocessing, alignment and peak-calling, followed by regulatory annotation.
