The Ensembl Rapid Release site makes new genomes available in as timely a manner as possible, with automated annotation of genes, etc, etc.

No variation, regulation or comparative data is provided for these species, and gene names have not been projected from human or other related organisms. However we do provide tools such as BLAST to help you to explore the genome and its annotation further.

New genomes are added approximately twice a month, depending on availability of resources.
