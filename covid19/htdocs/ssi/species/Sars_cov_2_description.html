Coronaviruses are a large family of enveloped, positive-sense, single-stranded RNA viruses that infect a broad range of vertebrates. This site represents the Severe acute respiratory syndrome coronavirus 2 (SARS-CoV-2) genome, responsible for the covid-19 pandemic, embedded within the Ensembl suite of tools.
