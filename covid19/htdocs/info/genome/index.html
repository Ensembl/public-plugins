Annotation & Prediction

Genes and transcripts

Annotation of the SARS-CoV-2 genome have been generated using a modified Ensembl genebuild supported by protein evidence. Further details are available on the SARS-CoV-2 annotation page.

Additional viral genomes and their annotation have been imported from ENA to allow comparative analysis with SARS-CoV-2.

Variation

Variation data is available for SARS-CoV-2 only - details are available on the SARS-CoV-2 variation page.
